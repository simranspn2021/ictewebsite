About Us

Our Founders Mr. Ravindra Singh Arora and Mrs. Rajender Kaur started icte also Known as Institute of Computer Training & Education in year 2010 to help students grow in IT Field by Providing them Fundamental and Advance Computer Knowledge via Paid as well as Free Goverment Funded Courses.

Along the Way Providing Free Goverment Funded Courses like National Digital Literacy Mission, National Urban Livelihood Mission (NULM), Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Mission (PMKVY) and many more.

We intently focused on serving growth-oriented Professional and indi­viduals facing expanding requirements and challenges in this new regulatory era.
